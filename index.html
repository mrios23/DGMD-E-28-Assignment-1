My Journey to Development

My journey into software development began at the University of Connecticut (UCONN) as a Computer Science and Engineering major. In 2020, amist the pandemic, I was promoted to a Software Engineer. In this role, I primarily work on backend development using Java. I'm excited to take this course to gain more front end experience, and complete my HES Frontend Web Developer Certification!
